## Required Author Checks Before Submission

- Complete the full author list in final order.
- Complete affiliations for all authors.
- Add ORCID iDs if available.
- Confirm the corresponding author information.
- Replace funding placeholders with final funding text or a no-funding statement.
- Complete author-contribution statements after confirming the final author list.
- Confirm competing-interest, ethics, consent, data-availability, and code-availability statements.
- Check whether the journal submission system requires declarations to be entered manually in addition to uploading the manuscript files.
